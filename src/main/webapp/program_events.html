How was this website built – Visionhill May 2015 Hackathon

On 30th may 2015, a group of Visionhill members gathered in Oklahoma Christian University campus to work on the Visionhill’s Website in a Hackathon dubbed Visionhill Hackathon. We established a live online platform where we exchanged expertise with people from Norman, it took the whole morning to develop both the website content and to build the website. Visionhill main objective is to create an environment where Rwandan living in Oklahoma will meet and share their daily social and professional life. The website will help people to access information and contribute to the overall success to the community.
