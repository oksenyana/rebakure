Membership Information

  • Visionhill members are individual who believe in Visionhill objectives and vision
  • We have events on the third Saturday of every odd month, if you are in the Oklahoma area make sure to check our website for more information about our next event, we will be glad to have you. Contact us at info@visionhill.org for more information.
  • If you would like to become a member send us an email at membership@visionhill.org or contact Membership and Community Coordinator
  • If you don’t live in the state of Oklahoma, you can become a satellite member by sending an email to membership@visionhill.org.
    • Though, we strongly encourage you to start a similar organization where you live.
    • Members are encouraged to participate actively in Visionhill Oklahoma activities by hosting events, speaking, etc. It is in Visionhill’s commitment to invite professionals and give opportunity to members to improve their leadership, communications skills and community engagement while creating a vibrant community where they live.
  • Entry to the organization is marked by an admission to the Facebook Group by the Community and Membership Coordinator


    • Registered Member benefits

  • Opportunity to remotely impact the country and communities
  • Opportunity to become a Vision Hill Trust Fund Trustee and Senior Trustee
  • Opportunity to invest and contribute to enterprise creation through Vision Hill Investment Fund
  • Receive announcements about Vision Hill events and initiatives
  • Actively participate invision Hill Network, events (Workshops, Cookouts, Galas) and programs
  • Opportunity to give speech/presentation and/or invite speaker(s) in one of the Vision Hill Bi-Monthly Workshop
  • Be eligible for organizational-wide mobilization for member support in promoting your business venture, project or initiative
  • Be eligible for organizational-wide mobilization for member support and solidarity in major life events
