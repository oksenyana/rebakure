Leadership

Rebakure shall have a formal leadership to insure successful operations and activity oversight.

  • Coordinator/General and President
  • Coordinator/Technology and Vice President
  • Coordinator/Membership and Community
  • Coordinator/Finance and Documentation
  • Coordinator/Event and Programs

Advancement Council

The role of the advancement committee shall be to serve as a guardian of the organization in the advisory capacity working with the president primarily to designate the following president.

  • Past Presidents
  • Initiators
  • Elders Advisors
  • Supporters
Membership

Membership Information

  • Rebakure members are individual who believe in Rebakure objectives and vision
  • We have events on the third Saturday of every odd month, if you are in the Oklahoma area make sure to check our website for more information about our next event, we will be glad to have you. Contact us at info@rebakure.org for more information.
  • If you would like to become a member send us an email at membership@rebakure.org or contact Membership and Community Coordinator
  • If you don’t live in the state of Oklahoma, you can become a satellite member by sending an email to membership@rebakure.org. Though, we strongly encourage you to start a similar organization where you live.
  • Members are encouraged to participate actively in Rebakure Oklahoma activities by hosting events, speaking, etc. It is in Rebakure’s commitment to invite professionals and give opportunity to members to improve their leadership, communications skills and community engagement while creating a vibrant community where they live.

Current Members

  • Entry to the organization is marked by an admission to the Facebook Group by the Community and Membership Coordinator
