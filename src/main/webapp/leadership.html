Coordination Committee

VisionHill shall have a formal leadership to insure successful operations and activity oversight. The President/coordinator general shall formulate and announce annual program every year in consultation with the team.

  • Coordinator/General and President
  • Coordinator/Technology and Vice President
  • Coordinator/Membership and Community
  • Coordinator/Finance and Documentation
  • Coordinator/Events and Programs
  • Coordinator/VisionHill Member Fund RW
  • Coordinator/VisionHill Member Fund US

Advancement Council

The role of the advancement committee shall be to serve as a guardian of the organization in the advisory capacity working with the president primarily to designate the following president. The Council shall meet twice a year on the third week from 10AM to 12AM in June (To evaluate annual program progress) and in November (To Evaluate Annual Program and advise on the following year program)

  • VisionHill Past Presidents
  • VisionHill Initiators
  • VisionHill Elders Advisors
  • OKC/OC familia co-Presidents (Ex-officio)
  • Tulsa Rwanda Community President (Ex-officio)
